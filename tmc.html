About
The Mishra Corporation

The Mishra Corporation is a Robloxian multi-division digital media corporation known for operating several major broadcast networks within the Robloxian broadcasting industry. The corporation focuses on structured media production, digital journalism, and realistic broadcast simulation across its platforms.

Through its networks, The Mishra Corporation delivers live news programming, cultural media content, and modern digital reporting to audiences across Roblox. The corporation is recognized for its professional structure, cross-network collaboration, and commitment to high-quality media production.

The Mishra Corporation was founded with the goal of creating a realistic media environment that mirrors real-world broadcasting operations. TeamMishra operates with departments, production teams, leadership roles, and active projects that resemble professional media corporations.

Organization & Structure

TeamMishra is known for its structured internal organization. Members are divided into departments and production teams that work together on active projects. Leadership oversees network operations, production schedules, and collaboration between channels.

Community & Recruitment

The Mishra Corporation actively recruits members interested in media production, journalism, design, and development. TMC has participated in the Roblox Media Conference hosted by ABU Television.

Partners
Partnerships

Roblox Broadcasting Union

The Mishra Corporation maintains a partnership with the Roblox Broadcasting Union (RBU) — focusing on collaboration between broadcast organizations, shared media standards, and joint participation in Roblox media events. Through RBU, TeamMishra works alongside other broadcasters to promote higher-quality production and a more connected Roblox media community.
