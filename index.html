About
Who We Are

Inline is the primary production and broadcast brand of The Mishra Corporation. Founded in 2023 after separating from Tahoma Digital, Inline has established itself as a driving force in the Roblox media community.

We focus on original programming, live events, and broadcast projects that push the boundaries of what's possible in virtual media production.

Through our networks, we deliver news, entertainment, and community programming to audiences across the Roblox platform.
